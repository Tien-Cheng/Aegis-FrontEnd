Singapore, Singapore

Automated Drones for Track Maintenence

In Singapore, Aegis.ai was contracted by the Singapore Land Transport Authority to deploy a series of automated drones to perform track inspections in the MRT system. Putting our expertise to good use, we developed a state of the art A.I. system to detect, and even report track faults. By deploying a series of charging stations, and tracking beacons, the drones could operate 24/7, operating without the need for constant supervision. Our drones helped improve the efficiency of Singapore's metro system, reducing the occurrences of train breakdowns, ultimately benefiting thousands of commuters.

Smart Tools for Smart Companies

Munich, Bavaria, Germany

King of Retail

From their humble beginnings in 1996, König, once a small retail company in Munich, has grown into one of Germany's most recognizable retail chains. The secret to their success? Aegis's expertise. With the help of our talented consultants, König was able to integrate data analytics to better understand their customers. They were able to make use of our tools to strategically expand their influence and better cater to their consumers. As a result, they were able to massively increase their profit margins and expand their market share.

Sydney, NSW, Australia

Lawyer Up!

Sydney based law firm, V & A, stays ahead of the game by making use of Aegis's A.I. systems. With Aegis SmartParse, V & A can quickly scan through legal documents, and extract key insights with greater accuracy than a human. This saves V & A hundreds of hours of manual due diligence, allowing them to focus on what's important. V & A also makes use of a specially designed data analytics platform, which can forecast the results of a court case, allowing it's lawyers to strategize their cases. With these tools, V & A has been able to rise to become one of the top law firms in Australia.

Issan makes full use of Aegis's data analytics services to develop its world-class menu's by analysing how their customers like to eat.
